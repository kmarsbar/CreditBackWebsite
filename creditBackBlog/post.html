Get Credit for Your Unused Mobile Data with 2 Degrees

At 2 Degrees, we understand that you want to get the most value from your mobile plan. Now, you can get credit back for your unused mobile data, ensuring you never waste a byte. Here's how it works:

Simple Rates:

  • If you have just a mobile plan with 2 Degrees, you can get 50¢ per 100MB of unused data.
  • If you bundle your mobile and broadband services with 2 Degrees, you can get 75¢ per 100MB of unused data.

For example, if you have 1040MB of unused data:

  • With a mobile-only plan, you would receive $5.20.
  • With a mobile and broadband bundle, you would receive $7.80.

This new feature ensures that you get the most value out of your mobile plan, turning your unused data into real savings. Stay connected and save with 2 Degrees!

Opt-In for Rollover Data or Credit with 2 Degrees

At 2 Degrees, we offer flexible options to help you manage your unused data. You can choose to either roll over your unused data or get credit for it. Here's how you can make the most of this feature:

Rollover Data: Prefer to keep your unused data? Opt-in to our rollover feature and carry over your remaining data to the next month. This way, you'll have extra data available when you need it.

Credit Back: Alternatively, you can choose to receive credit for your unused data. This credit can be used towards your next bill, helping you save money.

Whether you choose to rollover your data or get credit, 2 Degrees gives you the flexibility to manage your mobile plan according to your needs. Make the most of your data with our convenient options.

"The opt-in feature for rollover data or credit is fantastic! I love having the choice to keep my unused data for the next month or get some money back. It's a win-win situation!" - Alex, 2 Degrees Customer

Inactive Accounts Policy: What You Need to Know

To ensure that our customers are making the most of their mobile plans, 2 Degrees has updated its policy on inactive accounts. Here's what you need to know:

Inactive Accounts: Accounts that have been inactive for 2 months will lose all available credits. This means that if you have not used your mobile service for two consecutive months, any credits you have earned from unused data will be forfeited.

We recommend staying active with your mobile plan to retain your credits and continue benefiting from our great rates and services. If you anticipate a period of inactivity, consider using your accrued credits before they expire.

Stay active and make the most of your mobile plan with 2 Degrees. For any questions or more information, feel free to contact our customer support.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I opt-in for the data rollover or credit feature?

A: You can opt-in through the 2 Degrees mobile app or by contacting customer service. Once opted in, you can choose to either roll over your unused data or receive credit for it.

Q: How long can I keep my rolled over data?

A: Rolled over data can be used in the following month. If you don't use it within that month, it will expire.

Q: What happens if I don't use my mobile service for two months?

A: If your account is inactive for two months, you will lose all accrued credits. Make sure to use your mobile service regularly to retain your credits.
