Opt-In for Rollover Data or Credit with 2 Degrees

At 2 Degrees, we offer flexible options to help you manage your unused data. You can choose to either roll over your unused data or get credit for it. Here's how you can make the most of this feature:

Rollover Data: Prefer to keep your unused data? Opt-in to our rollover feature and carry over your remaining data to the next month. This way, you'll have extra data available when you need it.

Credit Back: Alternatively, you can choose to receive credit for your unused data. This credit can be used towards your next bill, helping you save money.

Whether you choose to rollover your data or get credit, 2 Degrees gives you the flexibility to manage your mobile plan according to your needs. Make the most of your data with our convenient options.
