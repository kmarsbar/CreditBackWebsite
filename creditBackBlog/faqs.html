Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: How do I opt-in for the data rollover or credit feature?

A: You can opt-in through the 2 Degrees mobile app or by contacting customer service. Once opted in, you can choose to either roll over your unused data or receive credit for it.

Q: How long can I keep my rolled over data?

A: Rolled over data can be used in the following month. If you don't use it within that month, it will expire.

Q: What happens if I don't use my mobile service for two months?

A: If your account is inactive for two months, you will lose all accrued credits. Make sure to use your mobile service regularly to retain your credits.

How to Get Started

Interested in maximizing your mobile plan with 2 Degrees? Here's how you can get started:

  1. Sign Up: Visit our website or download the 2 Degrees app to sign up for a mobile plan.
  2. Choose Your Plan: Select the mobile plan that best fits your needs. Don't forget to check out our bundles for even more savings.
  3. Opt-In: Opt-in for the data rollover or credit back feature through the app or by contacting customer service.
  4. Stay Active: Use your mobile service regularly to retain your credits and make the most of your plan.

For any questions or assistance, please get in contact with our support team!
